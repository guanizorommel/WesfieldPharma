About Us

Westfield is one of the most respected pharmaceutical companies today offering healthcare business-to-business solutions and services. Established in 1994, Westfield stayed true to its business principle that creating lasting partnerships will be the driving force behind the growth of the company, its business and of its clientele. Westfield is committed to providing a one-stop-shop solution for businesses to expand their product portfolio by offering pharmaceutical products in whatever form.



Our Mission is to provide high quality and cost effective medicines, superior services and commitment to customers.



Our Vision is to become an essential business partner in the pharmaceutical industry in delivering healthcare solutions to Filipino people.
